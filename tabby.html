Tabby cats

Tabby cats are known for their common pattern, which consists of an "M" marking on their foreheads and stripes along their body. "Tabby" refers to a coat that can be seen in almost all breeds of cats. There are many types of tabby cats, and they can also vary in color. This page focuses on brown classic tabbies.
